CORE VALUES

Build around the core values of Delivery, Quality and Creativity, Ishi is a product design and engineering firm. Our primary focus is creating managed solutions and products that leverage high performance computing, big data and analytics.

At Ishi, Customer Value and Customer Intimacy are the key principles that guide the delivery and quality of our solutions. Understanding and delivering value to our customers is the central business skill of our teams and leaders. This manifests in our approach of prioritizing the delivery of highest value in the shortest time.
